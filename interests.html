I will also record a personal singing album just for fun! Note: my singing voice is not spectacular, and I do not plan on releasing this to the world.

I am fluent in English, primarily speak Cantonese at home, occasionally speak Vietnamese at home, and am learning Mandarin, Korean, and Spanish. Cantonese linguistics particularly interest me, as I am a heritage speaker, and knowing Cantonese has helped me learn Vietnamese and Korean more easily, since they share many similar-sounding vocabulary. Even more interesting is the phonological and vocabulary differences within different "accents" of Cantonese. As a member of the Hoa diaspora, I learned that my Cantonese makes me sound somewhat like a country bumpkin... Everyday, I am excited to learn something new!
